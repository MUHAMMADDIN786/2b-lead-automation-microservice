## ❓ FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

### Q: What is the phone call latency? Does it sound robotic?
**A**: By using state-of-the-art providers like Vapi.ai and Retell AI paired with high-quality models (like Deepgram Nova-2 for speech-to-text, and ElevenLabs for text-to-speech), the latency is reduced to under 800ms. It supports interruptions, backchanneling (e.g., "uh-huh", "got it"), and sounds remarkably human.

### Q: What are the running costs for AI Voice Agents?
**A**: Phone calls are priced by the minute. Total cost consists of: Telephony (~$0.01/min), Speech-to-Text (~$0.01/min), LLM token usage (~$0.02/min), and Text-to-Speech (~$0.05 - $0.15/min). Generally, it costs around $0.10 to $0.22 per minute of active conversation—far cheaper than human staff.

### Q: Can the agent make outbound cold calls?
**A**: Yes. However, outbound calling has different legal regulations (e.g., TCPA compliance in the USA). We can configure outbound dialers, but we must discuss legal requirements and safety filters before starting.
